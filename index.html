Find your balance at Element Springfield South, located in the heart of the city's Medical Mile and just blocks away from the Mercy and Cox South campuses. Wake up to our complimentary Rise breakfast, offering fruit, granola, a build-your-own-parfait Chobani Yogurt station and hot items. We look forward to helping you be your best self at Element Springfield South.
